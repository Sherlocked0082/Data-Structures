10.Full and Complete
A.Full BT-> A Tree of height h having maximum Number of Nodes
ie. h ,n=pow(2,h+1)-1

B.Complete BT->If a Binary Tree is represented in Array then there should not any blank spaces in btw elements
ie.A Complete BT of height h will be a full BT uptill h-1 height nad in last level the elements will be filled 
from Left to Right without skipping any elements  

A Full BT is always a Complete BT but Complete BT need not be a Full BT
